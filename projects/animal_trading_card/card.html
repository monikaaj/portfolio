Fresian horse

Fresian horses are beautiful and majestic animal, but did you know they need only three hours of sleep a day?

  • Scientific Name: Friesian
  • Average High: 160 centimeters
  • Average Lifespan: 25 years
  • Ointment: Black

The history of a race starts in Roman times. Fresians are well-known for their very good, calm temper and willing to cooperate with human. Due to this advantages they are often used for hipotherapy.
